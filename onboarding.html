Why Gen Z & Alpha Choose Crypto Over Coupons

Understanding the shift from Web2 to Web3 marketing. This is the future of customer engagement.

What is Web3?

Web2 (Current): Companies own your data. Centralized control. Think Facebook, Instagram.

Web3 (Future): YOU own your data and assets. Decentralized. Powered by blockchain.

Why It Matters: Gen Z/Alpha grew up with digital ownership (skins in Fortnite, NFTs). They expect to OWN what they earn.

Why Crypto > Coupons

Traditional Coupons: Single-use, expire, can't be traded. Boring.

Crypto Tokens ($Ember): Tradeable, valuable, social status. Exciting.

Real Talk: Would you rather get a $5 Starbucks coupon OR $5 in Bitcoin you can hold, trade, or flex? Gen Z picks crypto every time.

Airdrops = Viral FOMO

What's an Airdrop? Free crypto tokens sent to users. Creates urgency, scarcity, and social proof.

Why It Works: "I got 1,000 $Ember tokens!" = instant social media post.

Pro Tip: Schedule airdrops during campaign launch to trigger viral sharing. One airdrop can 10X your reach.

Solana's Social Dominance

Why Solana? Fastest blockchain. Massive community on Twitter/X, TikTok, Discord.

The Network Effect: When you launch on Solana, crypto enthusiasts automatically discover and share your campaign.

Earned Media Value: Every player sharing "$Ember hunt at Starbucks!" = free advertising. Solana community amplifies organically.
