Your 5-Step Onboarding Process

From signup to your first campaign in 30 days or less. Based on best practices from successful location-based AR marketing, including user consent, targeting, and iterative testing.

Sign Up & Get Your Free Demo

Action: Create account at vaultphoenix.com

What you get:

  • $100 in FREE $Ember tokens
  • Access to management system
  • Live demo links
  • Guidance on obtaining user consent for location tracking (essential for compliance and trust)

Timeline: 5 minutes

2

Test the Platform

Action: Try the $Ember Hunt app (mobile demo) and Management System (desktop)

What you learn:

  • Player experience + campaign control interface
  • How coin placement works
  • Analytics dashboard capabilities
  • Research target audience behaviors and locations for optimal engagement
  • Set clear campaign goals (e.g., foot traffic increase, brand awareness)

Timeline: 15 minutes

3

White-Label Deployment or SDK Integration

For Marketers: Use automated app builder in management system, point domain. Customize for client branding.

For Developers: Clone SDK repo, integrate components, customize branding. Test integrations thoroughly.

Best Practice: Ensure seamless user onboarding with in-app prompts explaining AR features and value.

Timeline: 24 hours (marketers) or 1-2 weeks (developers)

4

Place Your First Coins

Action: Use management system to drop $Ember coins at strategic locations

Choose: GPS (outdoor) or Beacons (indoor) based on campaign needs

Best practices:

  • High-traffic areas
  • Partner locations
  • Event venues
  • Test in real-world scenarios to ensure accuracy and engagement
  • Geo-clone successful setups for scalability

Timeline: 30 minutes

5

Launch & Monitor

Action: Go live, track analytics in real-time dashboard

Monitor:

  • Player activity
  • Coin collections
  • ROI metrics
  • Advertiser engagement
  • User feedback and iterate based on data

Optimize: Adjust coin placements based on performance data, scale successful elements.

Timeline: Ongoing
