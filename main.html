The Phoenix Token Evolution

Every player starts with Phoenix Tokens—your campaign currency that never changes value. Players collect Phoenix Tokens from your placed coins and redeem them for your rewards. But here's where it gets interesting: Phoenix Tokens can evolve into $Ember Tokens, our blockchain cryptocurrency launching November 1, 2025.

Phoenix Token

Campaign currency your players collect and redeem for rewards you define. Stable value, full control, perfect for driving foot traffic and engagement.

Evolution Process

Players who meet your criteria (purchases, visits, referrals) can evolve their Phoenix Tokens into $Ember Tokens. You control the requirements and conversion ratios.

$Ember Token

Blockchain cryptocurrency with real market value. Players can hold, trade, or sell their $Ember Tokens while continuing to engage with your campaigns.

This dual-token system gives you maximum flexibility: run traditional reward campaigns with Phoenix Tokens, or add crypto incentives with $Ember evolution. Your campaign, your choice, your control.
