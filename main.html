Dual Placement Technology: GPS & Beacons

Our revolutionary platform supports both GPS outdoor placement and Beacon indoor placement, giving you unprecedented flexibility in location-based marketing campaigns. Whether your customers are exploring city streets or navigating shopping malls, they can discover your crypto rewards anywhere.

GPS Outdoor Placement

Perfect for tourism, retail storefronts, event venues, and citywide campaigns. Drop coins at specific GPS coordinates and watch foot traffic soar as customers hunt for rewards.

Beacon Indoor Placement

Ideal for shopping malls, stadiums, airports, and large indoor venues. Place physical Beacon devices and customers collect coins by scanning them with their phones—GPS not required!

Mix & Match for Maximum Impact

Combine GPS and Beacon placement in a single campaign. Lead customers from outdoor GPS locations to indoor Beacon checkpoints, creating immersive treasure hunt experiences that drive unprecedented engagement.
